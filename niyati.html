Niyati

A Skincare Service

The Problem

Indian skin types are very diverse, and yet companies market most of their products to the masses without clarifying their target user. This leads to customers experiencing heavy trial and error to find the right product for their skin.

Misleading advertising and unawareness of the correct usage of skincare products exists for consumers seeking better products, due to a lack of transparency from brands. This leads to adverse skin reactions and long-term health risks.

Findings

Research was done on different skin types and existing regulations for product marketing. We went to skincare stores and malls to interview people on their experience with skincare.

From this point, we realised that each person has a unique need of their own. If they do not consult a dermatologist, they have no reliable source to get product recommendations from. Users want the one golden product that solves their issues without having to go through long research processes and consultations.

Then, we came up with a service that acts as the middleman between brands and customers. The skincare market is flooded with products that confuse people already. So, our service helps ease the consultation process by recommending existing products in the market that cater to our userâ€™s needs.

What Our Service Does Better

Niyati helps consumers navigate through trial and error and strives to remove the error from the equation. We aim to help our users be more aware of the products they use.

Users can get skincare solutions by visiting our store to consult a dermatologist and cosmetologist for personalised experiences. They can also get more information on skin products and the ingredients used in them on our website. Niyati aims to reduce misinformation along with trial and error in the skincare industry while empowering individuality and compassion.
