Graphic Cube

The Ultimate Travel Destination: Kapellbrück

The Brief

A cube representing a destination I want to visit: Kapellbrück (Chapel Bridge) in Lucerne, Switzerland, known for its history as the oldest wood covered bridge in the world and the series of paintings it showcases. Each face of the cube contains elements inspired by or related to the place.

The Faces

Face 1

The first letter of the destination, illustrated to capture the roof and tower of the bridge.

Face 2

Typography inspired by the geometric shapes found in the bridge.

Face 3

Iconography that represents the bridge as an abstract icon.

Face 4

A pattern made by tessellating the top view of the octagonal water tower situated next to the bridge.

Face 5

A collage drawn from one of the famous painting series found at the bridge called the “Dance of Death” series. 

Face 6

My personal interpretation of what the bridge would look like from within while visiting the place.
