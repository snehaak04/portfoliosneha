Bookish

A Study Session App

What is Bookish?

Bookish is an app designed after conducting research and observational studies in university study areas and arriving at a problem statement that students lack an organised, accessible and comfortable space which affects their productivity.

Bookish aspires to create a streamlined and structured system where students can manage their productivity wherever they are. It promotes the usage of university provided spaces for studentsâ€™ needs.


How it Came About

Study areas are one of the most commonly sought after places in colleges. This means that they are rarely empty for people who want to use the space for themselves.

Bookish helps students find study areas in their campus and reserve spots for when they want to use them.

The app is also connected to the study area, where the user can personalise their space and adjust their environment on spot to fit their comfort.
